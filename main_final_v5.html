About Us

BACKGROUND:

HR Brothers Water Contractors, founded by Mr. Habib Ur Rehman in 2006, is a leading company in Karachi that provides water solutions. The company started small but has grown to cover the entire city. They are known for their excellent customer service, making sure every client is well taken care of. HR Brothers focuses on meeting the specific needs of each customer, showing their commitment to good service. Over the years, they have become a well-known and trusted name in the water solution industry in Karachi. Their revenue is over 120 million per annum.

MISSION STATEMENT:

“Our mission is to expand our operations all over Pakistan to guarantee healthy and safety water that can be availed at an affordable price in a convenient manner.”

VISION STATEMENT:

“We are committed to providing crystal clear responsibly sourced water, enhancing the quality of life for generations to come.”

TARGET AUDIENCE:

HR Brothers Water Contractors focuses predominantly on the residential and commercial sectors within several key localities of Karachi, notably DHA, Clifton, Korangi, and Teen Hatti. These areas are specifically chosen due to their critical need for drinkable water, a challenge that HR Brothers is dedicated to addressing. Additionally, the company boasts an impressive roster of major clients, including the Pakistan Navy, Sui Southern Gas Company, and Imtiaz Supermarket, among others. This diverse client base underscores HR Brothers' capacity to cater to a variety of water supply needs, from residential to large-scale commercial and industrial requirements.

SEGMENTATION:

HR Brothers Water Contractors adeptly segments its market to effectively address the varying needs for purified water across different sectors. The segmentation strategy is diverse and well-defined, encompassing a range of key consumer groups:

  • Residential Consumers: Focusing on households, HR Brothers caters to the daily water needs of families, ensuring a consistent supply of purified water for drinking, cooking, and other domestic uses.
  • Commercial Businesses: This segment includes offices, small businesses, and service-oriented establishments where purified water is essential for both employees and customers.
  • Industrial Sector: HR Brothers serves various industries requiring large volumes of purified water for their manufacturing processes, operational needs, and employee usage.
  • Educational Institutions: Recognizing the critical need for safe drinking water in schools and universities, HR Brothers provides tailored solutions to meet the needs of students, faculty, and staff.

Through this strategic segmentation, HR Brothers effectively meets the specific water purification needs of each sector, ensuring quality service and customer satisfaction.
